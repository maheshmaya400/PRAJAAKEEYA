### 6. How is the 5-Step Model different from regular voting?

**EN:** Regular voting typically ends at "Election." The 5-Step Model adds ongoing
**Correction**, **Rejection**, and **Promotion** stages — emphasizing continuous citizen
engagement rather than a single decision every few years.
